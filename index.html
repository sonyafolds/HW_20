ROOT

THE MODERN SOLUTION FOR BEGGINER PLANT OWNERS .

Overview Roles Team Tools
We found that a common problem people face today is finding the time to care for their plants. Individuals at times purchase plants that need far more care than they are able to maintain. This creates an endless cycle of frustration and mass purchasing. Our team stepped back and wondered, what is it that consumers need so that they are able to keep up with the plants they already have, and allow for them to learn as they go? That is the genesis of “Root”, an application that notifies you when your plant needs your attention as well as suggests plants based on your availability/schedule.
